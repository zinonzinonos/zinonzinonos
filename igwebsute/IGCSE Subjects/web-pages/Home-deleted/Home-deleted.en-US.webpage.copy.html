Philosophy and Promise

At Foley's, each pupil follows a curriculum pathway tailored to their unique abilities, needs, and career aspirations. We encourage pupils to make aspirational choices and strive for success in all their endeavors. Our commitment lies in providing a comprehensive and well-rounded curriculum to all pupils.

Our goal is to cultivate adventurous individuals with qualities of character, resilience, and self-awareness essential for success. We hold high expectations and are dedicated to supporting pupils in becoming content, accomplished, and responsible young individuals.

Guidance for Choosing Options

The options process is a guided choice based on negotiation between the school, pupil, and home. The goal is to match available courses to the aptitude and interests of each pupil to maximize success at Key Stage 4.

When selecting their options, students should consider their talents, interests, and career aspirations. It's important not to be influenced by friends, favorite teachers, or the assumption that any course will be easy.

The Foley's path to help you choose your Options  

  • Form 4 Taster Day and Parents Information Afternoon
  • Subject talks in lessons
  • Careers activities during form time
  • Options interviews (by appointment)
  • ​Options Form submission deadline: 21st February

After submission, the Careers team will review the forms, finalize options, and proceed to design options blocks.
